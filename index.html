BUILT FOR SERIOUS FISHING

WELCOME

Pesci has been providing Canadians with high quality fishing rod reels since 1963. Our dedication to well-designed, dynamic, and effective fishing rod technologies has come to fruition in our latest reel: The Baitcaster.

The Baitcaster reel has several new features that are useful for beginner, intermediate and advanced fishermen. It is also designed to fit recreational and competitive purposes. We believe the Baitcaster reel to be perfect for all fishing styles and techniques. Scroll down to check out it's features.

FEATURES

  • Aluminum/Graphite Hybrid Body
  • Corrosion resistant construction and materials
  • 11-Ball Bearing System for smooth casting and retrieving
  • ZeroReverse one-way clutch built for “catching”, not just fishing
  • High Efficiency Gearing (HEG)
  • Super Free Bearing Supported Pinion Gear System (SF)
